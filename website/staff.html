Meet the Staff

PATS combines fantastic in-house teachers with numerous guest instructor residencies in the course of each year to maximize opportunities for learning and inspiration. The school is directed by **Meredith Klein**, who has been dancing tango for nearly 20 years. Her dance partner, **Andres Amarilla**, a Buenos Aires native and PATS co-founder, teaches with her during 5 months of the year. **Kristin Balmer**, an artist and tango teacher, does a phenomenal job with our weekly beginner classes. No one makes tango so immediately accessible and fun as Kristin does. **Amy Yang and Elly Fernandez** teach occasional classes for PATS. **Matthew McLaughlin** is our in-house photographer and lifter of heavy objects (like kegs!). Additional staff includes: **Alexei Borovik** (Classical Ballet) **Emiliano Messiez** (Piano - tango and other genres) See below for complete bios for each staff person, or skip to our [guest instructor residencies](/guest-teachers).

Philly Queer Tango

After a hiatus, Philly Queer Tango is back, guided by its founders Karen Johnson and Elly Fernandez. We are very happy to welcome PQT, which will be hosting once monthly events at the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School! In the City of Brotherly Love, Philly Queer Tango welcomes all who are interested in exploring open role tango in an LGBTQI friendly environment. Make friends, learn to dance, and participate in creating a diverse, welcoming, positive tango community. Come join us to learn and practice this improvised dance in the role of your choosing. Light refreshments included. Saturday, September 14: 2-3 pm: Introductory Lesson 3 pm-?: Stay to dance, ask questions, hang out Cost: $10

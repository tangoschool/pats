Milongas & Practicas

We hold a weekly milonga on Wednesday nights, and our big monthly milonga on the 4th Saturday of each month. We also offer a real PRACTICING practica on the first & third Sundays of each month. __Wednesdays__ __9 pm-midnight:__ Palermo Fishtown Milonga, preceded by two classes. Milonga costs $10, but is free for students who take one of the previous classes, except on live music nights. __1st & 3rd Sundays of the month__ __4-6 pm:__ Bailongo de los domingos Practica, a real PRACTICING practica. $10 __4th Saturdays of the month:__ __8:30-9:30 pm:__ Pre-milonga Class __9:30 pm-2 am:__ Milonga Qilombo with excellent DJs, often with live music Except where otherwise noted, all activities takes place at __Philadelphia Argentine Tango School__ 2030 Frankford Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125

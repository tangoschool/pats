The Socially-Distanced Outdoor Concert Series continues on Saturday, November 14 at 2 pm with a concert of Classical & New Music. **PERFORMERS** [Emiliano Messiez](/emiliano), piano and series curator [Amy Kang](http://www.amykang.com/), cello ## [View the Program](https://drive.google.com/file/d/10CAHdZmPNMLQV0bibDnKU6Tk_0nFEuHQ/view?usp=sharing) **LOCATION** Greensgrow Farms, 2501 E Cumberland St, Philadelphia, PA 19125 **PRECAUTIONS** Masks, distancing and adherence to all safety protocols are mandatory. Registrants are required to sign and return [this agreement](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1z0CqDlY-ND2SuLSlS7-8AAssRAM4_WQo/view?usp=sharing) in advance of the concert. It goes without saying that there is NO dancing of any kind at any socially-distanced concert. The greenhouse has a system of fans that pull outside air continuously through the space to mimic a completely outdoor environment. The greenhouse will have added plastic walls to maintain the temperature for the plants by the November concert. But the air flow will still be like outdoors. Combined with masks and distance, we are confident that this is safe. ### [View photos from the first concert](https://www.flickr.com/photos/51619376@N03/albums/72157716210803191) ### [View a video of the first concert](https://youtu.be/wfP-2amqlFs) **IN REAL LIFE & BY LIVESTREAM** 2 pm concert **REGISTRATION OPTIONS** Attend live: $40 Attend the livestream virtual event: $15 ## [REGISTER](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopOfferingsByDate&searchText=amy&offerTypeFilter=Drop%20In%20Class,Workshop) If outside the U.S., you can pay for the livestream using paypal: https://paypal.me/philadelphiatango **NOTES FOR THE LIVE EVENT** - No walk-ins will be allowed.

Youth Tango at PATS

**Youth Tango in 2019** We are working on starting a Youth Tango program at PATS. Depending on kids' availability, this may take the form of a weekend retreat (usually a Saturday or Sunday from 2-4 pm), or a weekly class (45 minutes to 1 hour). We are targeting the classes to two different ages groups: Teens: ages 12-17 Kids: ages 7-11 If your child or teen may be interested in participating, please email meredithklein@gmail.com! **Benefits of the Youth Tango Program** Youth tango at PATS is not just about learning steps! Our interdisciplinary classes integrate this unique partner dance with Improvisation and Leadership Skills development. The benefits of dancing tango are many - youth will cultivate self-confidence, boost their verbal and non-verbal communication skills, engage their sense of belonging, foster awareness of the space around them, and explore various ways to solve problems on and off the dance floor. We strongly believe in playfully exploring where this remarkable dance can take us - come join the fun! Taught by [Kristin Balmer](/kristin) & [Elly Fernandez](/elly).

Tipica Messiez at Lincoln Center

After its overwhelmingly successful debut at the [9th Philly Tango Fest](http://www.philadelphiatangofestival.com), the [Típica Messiez](https://www.emilianomessiez.com/tipica) is about to play its second concert: at Lincoln Center as part of the [Midsummer Night Swing series](https://www.lincolncenter.org/midsummer-night-swing/show/tipica-messiez-1)! Consider joining us on Thursday, July 11 as we travel to support this amazing new orchestra, whose launch was made possible by the largely Philadelphia-based donors to the [Típica Campaign](https://fundraising.fracturedatlas.org/the-philadelphia-argentine-tango-school/campaigns/2486). Make your way there on your own, or join us on a chartered coach from the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School! (We will need 40 registrants in order to confirm the bus. If we can't get there, we will refund your payment.) If you're interested, please sign up soon - and tell your friends! **3 pm:** Bus leaves from Philadelphia Argentine Tango School, 2030 Frankford Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125 **6 pm or before:** Arrive Lincoln Center **6 pm:** Dance floor opens **6:30–7:15 pm:** Group dance lesson **7:30–8:30 pm:** First live set - Típica Messiez **9-10 pm:** Second live set - Típica Messiez **10:30 pm:** Leave for Philadelphia (Schedule subject to change. Please see note about inclement weather below.) **Cost:** $55/person, includes transportation and ticket to the concert / social dance [REGISTER NOW](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopOfferingsByDate&searchText=lincoln&offerTypeFilter=Drop%20In%20Class,Workshop) **Please note:** This is an outdoor event and may be cancelled due to inclement weather. We are working on a plan to go to a milonga in NYC in case the Lincoln Center event is rained out. If it rains enough to cancel the event, registrants could choose EITHER to go to NYC anyways with us and attend a class & milonga, OR receive an $18 refund (the Lincoln Center ticket price). If we go to a milonga that is not Lincoln Center, we will leave for Philadelphia at midnight. The chartered bus will not allow us to cancel at the last minute without losing the entire cost, so full refunds will not be possible. Bottom line: if you have any influence with the weather gods, please use it!

Corina Herrera & Ines Muzzopappa

Corina Herrera & Ines Muzzopappa will teach TOGETHER in Philly for the first time from Monday, April 15 through Wednesday, April 17. Don't miss the chance to study with one of the most electrifying tango couples currently touring. Offerings include group classes & private lessons. __MONDAY, APRIL 15__ __7-8 pm:__ Tango Technique: Individual and personal work. Building our axis, sharing weight and looking for groundedness. Walking and pivots. Quality and subtlety. (All levels, individuals welcome) __8-9:30 pm:__ Boleos: The proposal and interpretation. Different types and qualities (Partners-Only) *If you are interested in taking this class, but don't have a partner, Karen Johnson will be keeping a list of people who are looking. Her contact info is below.* __WEDNESDAY, APRIL 17__ __7-8 pm:__ The embrace, searching the different positions, how to adapted to find a good sensation. (Intermediate Tango. Partner Rotation. Everyone practices both roles.) __8-9 pm:__ Introduction to the tango embrace. First movements to start improvising (Beginner Tango. Partner Rotation. Everyone practices both roles.) __9-midnight:__ Palermo Fishtown Milonga We will honor our regular pricing, with the caveat that classes will not be 2-for-1 on Wednesday night. [Register for group classes.](https://philadelphiatangoschool.cowtinker.com/schedule) __Private Lessons__ If you can possibly take private lessons with both Ines & Corina while they are in Philly, you may end up saying at the end of the 2024 that it was the single biggest thing that improved your dance this year. __Lessons are available:__ Monday, April 15: Late afternoon, early evening Tuesday, April 16: 11 am to 9 pm Wednesday, April 17: 11 am to 4 pm, and 5-7 pm. Private lesson slots are limited. They don't want to fill all of those hours. So please register soon. __Costs:__ Ines OR Corina - 1 hour: $120 Ines AND Corina - 1 hour: $160 *Payment is in cash, directly to Ines and Corina.* __To schedule:__ Karen Johnson has volunteered to schedule and manage the private lessons for Ines & Corina.

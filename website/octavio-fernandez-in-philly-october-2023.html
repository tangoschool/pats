Octavio Fernandez in Philly

For me, Octavio Fernandez is simply one of the greatest tango dancers in the world. He makes use of a remarkable range of movement qualities and dynamics to craft one of the most satisfyingly musical expressions of tango, and all in pure improvisation. As a teacher, performer, DJ and as a really great person to be around, he is just amazing. We are so grateful to be able to host him in Philadelphia for a week this October. Offerings include our regular group classes, private lessons, a special day of workshops co-taught with Carla Marano, and his phenomenal DJing too!

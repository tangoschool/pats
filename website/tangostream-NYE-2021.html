Tangostream: NYE 2021

**Post-Event Note:** Happy New Year, everyone! Thank you so much to everyone who celebrated with us during our epic 11-hour event. It really was the perfect way to say goodbye to 2020 and usher in a year that we hope will bring us back tango. Thanks especially to the donors who gave so generously. We've decided to prepare a special livestream for the donors, showing some of the highlights of the TANGOSTREAM event. More info on that coming very soon!

Produced by Leandro Ragusa & Meredith Klein. ## JUAN VILLARREAL & PATRICIO CROM ### A Livestream Concert to celebrate International Tango Day We enjoyed a beautiful live concert from Buenos Aires on December 11, 2020 by Juan Villarreal & Patricio Crom to celebrate International Tango Day. [Learn more](Live-Concert-International-Tango-Day-2020). ## A NIGHT WITH LISANDRO ADROVER ### A Concert & Interview with genius of the bandoneon, Lisandro Adrover Oliver Kolker and Meredith Klein produced a solo bandoneon album, [Bandoneon Soliloquy](https://bandoneonsoliloquy.com/), by legendary composer/performer Lisandro Adrover, during the pandemic. They presented the album release event - a concert, interview and live Q & A - on November 7, 2020. We are happy to make this album release event available on demand.

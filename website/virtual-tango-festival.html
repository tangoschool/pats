Virtual Tango Festival

The **Virtual Tango Festival** took place from May 22-25, 2020, and included thirty-five events: group classes (for individuals and couples), lectures, and concerts - all offered online. We are not planning to hold a Virtual Tango Festival in 2021. **RESOURCES FOR REGISTRANTS:** * [Advice for the Best Virtual Tango Fest experience](/virtual-tango-festival-zoom-help) - How to access help before and during the festival * [Virtual Artists Table](/virtual-tango-festival-virtual-artists-table) - Learn more about the artists' ongoing online activities * [What You Need for Special Activities](/virtual-tango-festival-special-activities) - If you are taking yoga, the bookmaking workshop, or the Nonviolent Communication Workshop, please have these items on hand. **NOTE: For most registrants, your zoom links are in the receipt(s) that got emailed to you when you registered.** If you registered before we had the zoom links figured out, you have received a personal email from me with your links. If you can't find the email, check your spam and promotions folders. You can also [login to your yogareg account](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopOfferingsByDate&offerTypeFilter=Drop%20In%20Class,Workshop) to view your receipt. Check out the bios/photos of our truly amazing [Presenters](/virtual-tango-festival-presenter-bios). View the Virtual Tango Festival [Press Release](https://drive.google.com/file/d/13vjYfegDUchsXXwh_nLp4KaC_QmZ40Xs/view?usp=sharing) The grid below includes all festival offerings. **The time zone is New York (UTC/GMT minus 4 hours).**

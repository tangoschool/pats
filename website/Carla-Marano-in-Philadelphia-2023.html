Carla Marano in Philly

Group classes and workshops for Carla Marano's residency in Philadelphia are outlined below. Please scroll further for individual class and pass pricing, and private lesson information. -------------------------- # Mondays, January 9-30 Hybrid: Available in-studio and online ### 7-8 pm: Tango Technique (All levels, both roles) - __January 9:__ “Cadencia” of the walk: How to vary the cadence, depending on the rhythm we want to use. Footwork: how and where to place the foot in each step; alignment of the legs. - __January 16:__ Free leg vs. Standing Leg. Finding the balance between freedom and stability. Adornments with different dynamics and intensities. - __January 23:__ Exploring the Diversity of Rotations. Exploring the motor and the source of the spirals that create pivots. Back & Forward ochos. - __January 30:__ Giros / Molinetes. The “Code of March” (pattern of the turn) in linear and circular forms. How step size changes with speed and context. ### 8-9:30 pm: Partners-Only Tango (Intermediate / Advanced Level) - __January 9:__ Walking patterns with rhythmic changes and “changes of front.” Alignment of the legs in each step that we take together. - __January 16:__ Paradas with challenging embellishmnets for both roles, plus a few sacadas - __January 23:__ Everything about Ochos: how to lead them, how to do them (for both roles). Sacada combinations in forward and back ochos for both roles. - __January 30:__ Turns – Changes of Direction through / with rebounds We will create change-of-direction combination with both paradas and sacadas -------------------------- # Wednesdays, January 4-February 1 Available in-studio only Everyone practices both roles ### 7-8 pm: Intermediate Tango - __January 4:__ Dance Floor Navigation, using basic sequences”¦ - __January 11:__ From the Calesita to the Circular Boleo: For the leader, steps around the follower; for the follower, spirals. Open & close embrace. - __January 18:__ (The Easiest) Turns with Enrosques - __January 25:__ Milonga: Smooth and with Traspie - __February 1:__ Back Sacadas for Leaders and Followers ### 8-9 pm: Beginner Tango (Start Any Week!) - __January 4:__ Basic Step, Technique, Connection, Use of Space - __January 11:__ Basic Musicality in the Walk, Basic Step, Front Ocho - __January 18:__ Basic Step, Back & Forward Ochos - __January 25:__ An Introduction to Milonga (the dance) with rebounds - __February 1:__ An Introduction to Tango turns (giros) ### 9 pm-midnight: Palermo Fishtown Milonga Friendly environment. Great music. Food & drink. Free with purchase of either class. $10 otherwise. __1/4:__ DJ Carla Marano __1/11:__ DJ Kerry Kay __1/18:__ DJ Jorge Fernandez -------------------------- # Saturdays, January 7-28 ### 2-3 pm: Special Seminar Exploring the Role of the Follower in Tango All levels. All genders welcome. - In this four-class series, we will explore: - Technique for standing and connecting - Technique for walking in high heels - Balance - Posture - Delicacy of Movements - Elegance - How to develop class and personal style in our dance ### 3-4 pm: Four Week Seminar on Improvisation Intermediate / Advanced level. - Lead & Follow Technique - Understanding structure - Exercises to improve improvisation ### 4-5 pm: Guided Practica Free with purchase of one or both classes. $10 otherwise. # Saturday, January 28 ## Milonga Qilombo 8:30-9:30 pm: Pre-Milonga Class 9:30 pm-1 am: Milonga Qilombo

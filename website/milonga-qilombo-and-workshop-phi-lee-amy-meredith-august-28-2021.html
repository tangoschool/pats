August 2021 Milonga Qilombo Weekend: Workshop + Milonga

We are thrilled to have Phi Lee Lam of the Queer Tango Collective (NYC) in Philadelphia for a workshop and milonga on Saturday, August 28. ### Saturday, August 28 __6-7:30 pm: Bodies in Motion: Transcending Dichotomies__ A workshop with Phi Lee Lam, Amy Yang & Meredith Klein In this workshop, Phi Lee, Amy and Meredith will give participants tools and space to play with tango movement. We'll disentangle tango movement from the dichotomies that usually govern the way we dance and think tango: male/female and lead/follow. We'll also re-examine the idea that "it takes two." Join us for an hour and a half of play, exploration and discovery. Intermediate level & up. $25 [Please pre-register](/registration). This is a partner change workshop.

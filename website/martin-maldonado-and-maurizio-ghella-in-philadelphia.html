Martin Maldonado & Maurizio Ghella in Philadelphia!

Martin Maldonado & Maurizio Ghella, both from Argentina, currently live and work in Vienna, Austria. In addition to their academic training in tango dance, they have studied tango in Buenos Aires with the original milongueros. They are representatives and carriers of the tradition and culture of authentic Argentine tango, and are sharing this knowledge and skills with the rest of the world. [Read their biography here.](/martin-maldonado-maurizio-ghella-bio) We were fortunate to have them teach & perform in Philadelphia in 2012, and we have been anxiously waiting for them to come back ever since.

Please put "Lisandro" in the notes so we know what your payment is for. ## A NIGHT WITH LISANDRO ADROVER - FULL VIDEO ## Who is Lisandro Adrover? Lisandro Adrover is one of the great bandoneonists and tango composers of our time. He has enjoyed a successful career as a performer, sharing the stage with Roberto Goyeneche and Osvaldo Pugliese, among many other tango greats, and has toured throughout the world. His music has been featured in films like The Impostors (1998) and Fermin (2014). He was a cast member of "Tango Argentino," widely regarded as the show that renewed interest in tango worldwide. He is the composer and arranger for the Broadway and London hit show, "Forever Tango." He has composed major works for and appeared as soloist with symphony orchestras, including conducting and recording with the Metropole Orchestra of the NPS Radio Netherland. [Read Lisandro's full bio](lisandro-adrover-bio) ## The Project Oliver Kolker & Meredith Klein were working on several collaborations early this year, all of which became impossible due to Covid. We started looking for a place to channel our collective energy that would be achievable during the pandemic, and given the fact that Buenos Aires was in very strict lockdown. Entranced by Maestro Adrover's exquisite bandoneon performance and varied repertoire, we asked him to record a solo concert and interview to be presented in an online event. Both were expertly filmed by Yael Szmulewicz and her team in an exquisite antique home in the San Telmo neighborhood of Buenos Aires.

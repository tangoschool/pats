Advanced Beginner Intensive (Level 2)

The Advanced Beginner Intensive is for students who have taken our Beginner Intensive, our weekly beginner classes (approximately 8 weeks), or have equivalent experience In addition to reviewing and solidifying material from the beginner level, the Intensive covers topics crucial in preparing students for our regular Intermediate-level group classes. Topics include: cross system, the cross basic step, the sandwich (both sides), simple barridas, front and back ochos, and simple walks in parallel and cross system.

Trabajadores del Tango Danza: Food Distribution Campaign

Through the generosity of over 100 donors, the Philadelphia tango community raised $5,810 to support food distributions efforts to tango professionals in need in Buenos Aires. The fundraiser was created and supported by the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School, Lori Coyle/Sangha Space, Krissy McFadden/Philly Tango, Maria Aldrete and Filippa DePaolo. While Philly initiated, donors gave from around the world. On Friday, September 18, $5,035 was delivered in cash to Trabajadores del Tango Danza, enough money to support their current level of food distribution for the next seven months!

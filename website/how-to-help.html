How to Help

Tango teachers, organizers and musicians are experiencing a totally unprecedented and unexpected time of no, or severely limited, income. Despite our active schedule of online classes, the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School is experiencing a 90% reduction in income due to Covid-19. We have been spending much of our time applying for every funding opportunity available. We are very fortunate to have received the commitment for a $5,000 grant from the City of Philadelphia. We hope to have more good news to share with you soon. Meanwhile, if you'd like to help: * Consider joining our [online tango classes](online-class-offerings) and [our online lecture series](lecture-series-via-zoom-Wednesdays). You can also take online tango piano lessons with Emiliano! [Email Meredith](mailto:meredithklein@gmail.com) to set it up! * Consider making a [tax-deductible donation](https://fundraising.fracturedatlas.org/the-philadelphia-argentine-tango-school) to the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School through our fiscal sponsor, Fractured Atlas. * Consider giving a gift certificate. You can [purchase on our registration site](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=loginform). We will email you a PDF gift certificate to print out or email to your giftee! * Consider [pre-purchasing group or private lessons](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopbundles) with Andres, Kristin or Meredith to use when the school is open again. You can also pre-purchase piano lessons with Emiliano at the same link! Class cards purchased now will be good for one year or longer. Class cards already purchased will be extended by the length of this crisis. * Consider [submitting a registration for the 2020 festival](https://www.philadelphiatangofestival.com/registration), which will be transferred to the 2021 festival, when the plan is finalized. When we re-open registration for the re-scheduled festival, prices will have to be higher. So you can save money and help us enormously by registering for the 2020 festival, even though it is not actually happening until 2021. * If there is any way that you can ask us for an account credit instead of a refund for any activity, you will help us enormously and we will be forever grateful. * We have virtual volunteer opportunities available, largely involving helping to promote our online activities. Please [email Meredith](mailto:meredithklein@gmail.com) if you'd like to be involved in this way. * Donate to an organization working on a vaccine. As much as I would like to think otherwise, it may be that tango cannot return to its previous level of activity unil we have one.

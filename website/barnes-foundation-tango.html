Tango at the Barnes Foundation: Friday, January 3

We are thrilled to be working with the Barnes Foundation to start 2020 off with a live music tango event. The event features live tango music, performed by [Emiliano Messiez](https://www.emilianomessiez.com/) (piano and direction), Javier Sanchez (bandoneon) and [Pablo Lanouguere](https://www.pablolanouguere.com/) (bass), including two full hours of live music for social dancing. If you've already danced at the Barnes, then you know that their floor is among the most comfortable for dancing in the entire city! (It's reclaimed wood from the Coney Island boardwalk, and it's heaven to dance tango on.) Also offered as part of the event are: a beginner tango lesson and a tango performance. If you are on our mailing list, you'll be getting a discount code in the mail. If you're not yet on the mailing list, you can join [here](/sign-up). **Schedule:** Friday, January 3, 2020 6-6:30 pm: Beginner Tango Lesson (everyone welcome!) 6:30-7:30 pm: Social Dancing with Live Music by the [Messiez](/emiliano) Tango Trio 7:30-7:50 pm: Tango Performance by [Andres Amarilla](/andres) & [Meredith Klein](/meredith), and invited dancers 7:50-8:50 pm: More Social Dancing with Live Music [Buy tickets!](https://www.barnesfoundation.org/whats-on/first-friday-tango) Your ticket includes entrance to the collections. **Location:** Barnes Foundation, 2025 Benjamin Franklin Parkway, Philadelphia, PA 19130

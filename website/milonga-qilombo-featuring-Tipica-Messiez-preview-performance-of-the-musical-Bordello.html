Milonga Qilombo feat.Típica Messiez | Sat. November 23

The Típica Messiez returns to Philadelphia for Milonga Qilombo at the Lithuanian Music Hall on Saturday, November 23. Adam Hoopengardner & Ciko Tanik will be visiting to teach and DJ. This very special night also features a preview performance of music from the upcoming musical, **Bordello**, by Barbara Bellman and Emiliano Messiez. **8-8:30 pm: First Look Reception.** Meet the creative team behind the upcoming musical, Bordello, including Barbara Bellman (book & lyrics) and Emiliano Messiez (music). The VIP ticket includes entrance to this open bar reception, a flash drive with demos of the gorgeous music of Bordello, and special seating during the milonga. **8:30-9:30 pm: All-Levels Tango Class.** Taught by [Adam Hoopengardner & Ciko Tanik](adamandcikotango.com). No experience or partner needed. **9:30 pm-2 am: Milonga Qilombo.** Featuring live music by the [Típica Messiez](https://www.emilianomessiez.com/tipica), co-DJs Adam & Ciko. At 11:15 pm, we'll have a 15-minute preview performance of music from the upcoming musical, **Bordello**. The performance includes musicians, dancers (Andres Amarilla & Meredith Klein) and a singer. Don't miss the chance to be among the first to experience this amazing show! **Location:** Lithuanian Music Hall, 2715 E.

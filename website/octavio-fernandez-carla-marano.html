Octavio Fernandez & Carla Marano in Philadelphia

Octavio Fernandez & Carla Marano have visited PATS multiple times over the past three years. We can honestly say that they are two of the best tango teachers in the world today. Their precision as dancers and teachers, knowledge of body mechanics, crystal-clear accuracy about musicality, and extreme subtlety in lead-and-follow communication, make them an equal pleasure to study with and watch perform.

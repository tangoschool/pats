Beginner Intensive: March 7 & 8

The Beginner Intensive covers the basics of tango vocabulary, structure, technique and lead-and-follow improvisation. We change partners after every song, so there's no need to bring a partner (though you're welcome to if you have one!). The Intensive prepares students to enter directly into our Advanced Beginner Level. This intensive is open to students with no previous experience in tango. Students with experience may also take this intensive to review and reinforce the basics.

Natalia & Agustin in Philly: May 1-28

### WEEKLY GROUP CLASSES *These classes are open role. Everyone is welcome to work on the role that they prefer. Trying both roles is encouraged.* **WEDNESDAYS, MAY 1-22** **7:30-9 pm:** Intermediate/Advanced Level. *$25 or [use class card](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopbundles)* Weekly topics: * May 1: Analysis of D’Arienzo's history to understand his music and how to dance to him * May 8: Analysis of Troilo's history to understand his music and how to dance to him * May 15: Analysis of Calo's history to understand his music and how to dance to him * May 22: Embellishments to match the lightness or heaviness of the song **9-midnight:** Milonga El Puntazo. *Included with class. $10 otherwise* **MONDAYS, MAY 6-20** **7-8 pm:** Tango Technique. *All Levels. $10* Weekly topics: * May 6: Basics of relaxed embrace and fluidity * May 13: Basics of increasing and decreasing tension in the dance, and how this is useful in the dance * May 20: Contrasting tension and relaxation **8-9:30 pm:** Intermediate Tango. *$25 or [use class card](https://secure.yogareg.com/philadelphiatango/pub?action=shopbundles)* Weekly topics: * May 6: Advanced technique for relaxed embrace and fluidity * May 13: Advanced technique for increasing and decreasing tension in the dance, and how this is useful in the dance * May 20: How the contrast between tension and relaxation play out in an outstanding, expressive dance ### SPECIAL EVENTS **Saturday, May 11** Last concert of the [Fishtown Salon Series 2018-19](/fishtown-salon) **8-9:30 pm:** Concert by Jazz Pianist [Dave Burrell](/fishtown-salon) **9:30 pm-midnight** Milonga with DJs Agustin & Natalia **May 24-27** [9th Philly Tango Fest](http://www.philadelphiatangofestival.com), featuring 14 maestros, 6 DJs, and the launch of the Orquesta Tipica Messiez. Pre-register by May 20 to benefit from package pricing. At the door, you may only purchase individual events. Natalia & Agustin are teaching, DJing, and performing at the festival!

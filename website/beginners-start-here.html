Beginners, Start Here!

We offer a drop-in class for beginners every Wednesday at 8 pm. New students may start any week. Many of our more experienced students attend to help beginners, which makes the learning more fun. The Beginner class is followed by a milonga (tango social dance) until midnight. ### Wednesday Schedule __8-9 pm:__ Beginner Tango - start any week - FREE for anyone ages 30 and under __9 pm-Midnight:__ Palermo Fishtown Milonga __Wednesday costs & promos:__ - One class - $25 or use your class card or monthly pass - Pay for the intermediate class and get the rest of the night free, except on live music nights. - Pay for the beginner class and get the rest of the night free, except on live music nights. - The Beginner class is free if you are age 30 or under.

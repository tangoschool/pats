NYC Invades Philly

*Dance like in NYC, but without the drive, when NYC invades Philly over the weekend of January 26 & 27!* The weekend features Adam Hoopengardner & Ciko Tanik, who are excellent dancers, passionate and approachable teachers, and super-fun people to be around. They will also be featured maestros during the **9th Philly Tango Fest** and you can read their full bios and view videos on the [festival website](https://www.philadelphiatangofestival.com/adam-hoopengardner-and-ciko-tanik). We are also pleased to introduce a new NYC-based DJ, Kim Dai, who will be DJing in Philly for the first time. **Saturday, January 26** **Location:** Lithuanian Music Hall Association, 2715 E. Allegheny Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19134 **8:30-9:30 pm:** All-levels pre-milonga class – Adam & Ciko **9:30 pm-2 am:** Milonga Qilombo with DJ Kim Dai (NYC) and a performance by Adam & Ciko **Sunday, January 27** **Location:** Philadelphia Argentine Tango School, 2030 Frankford Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125 **2-3:30 pm:** Delayed Sacadas – He goes… She goes… He goes. This class will offer something for everyone, including those with 3 months or 10 years of sacada experience. It will change your usual dynamics and musicality for sacadas, plus it will enhance your technique with turns and positions within the turn, making both your turns and sacadas more efficient! *Open workshop with Partner Rotation. (Level: Advanced Beginner & Up)* **4-5:30 pm:** Creating a Deeper, Advanced Dialogue between Leader & Follower. Focusing on followers’ ability to suggest ideas, to communicate without back-leading. And on leaders’ ability to listen and respond without losing decisiveness as a leader. *Partners-Only Workshop (Level: Intermediate & Up)* **Pricing** *Pre-registration is now closed. You are welcome to pay for all activities at the door.* One workshop: $35 Two workshops: $55 Milonga Qilombo + class: $20 Milonga Qilombo only: $15

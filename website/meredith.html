Meredith Klein

Meredith Klein has been dancing tango for 27 years, including three years spent living in Buenos Aires. Meredith directs the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School (since 2008), the [Philadelphia Tango Festival](http://www.philadelphiatangofestival.com) (since 2010), [Milonga Tours](http://www.milongatours.com) (offering tango tours of Buenos Aires for dancers since 2009), and the tango record label [Bochinche Records](http://www.bochincherecords.com) (since 2023). Before tango, Meredith came from a background in music, and has made it her life's mission to help tango dancers and tango musicians understand each other a little better. Her Bochinche Records released [Psicoporteño](https://open.spotify.com/album/3fYvbjnNKO1H1ukow19eMb?si=nkIrjvTgR8OqXX_UXTJEWg) by Tipica Messiez in June 2023. The next year, Bochinche released [La inevitable tentacion de ir a contramano](https://open.spotify.com/album/1qtsbKPLRxxlLJfWV8tuqn?si=zfdNHCjrQ4Cn-mIC4pk1ng) by Sexteto Fantasma, which was nominated for a [Latin Grammy Award for "Best Tango Album."](https://www.latingrammy.com/en/artists/sexteto-fantasma/59629-01). On December 11, 2025, Bochinche released the first triple album in the history of tango, [Iconico y Barrial](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/55Y01ic1NXETasOwRWfmHX?si=efb343310c02494b) by Orquesta Misteriosa Buenos Aires. In 2026, Bochinche Records has released three new albums. [Urban Tango](https://open.spotify.com/album/0u73FiGnZ5q9jrZWsHt0Dy?si=LYYe9NRQTXKfaV2Fna1ssA) by Pianist Mariano Castro was actually recorded in the studio of the Philadelphia Argentine Tango School in January 2026. It was released in March 2026. [Cruce](https://open.spotify.com/album/1jxQYBMa1jVfxAVg90auFZ?si=e72iM1TeTiyygCU9xR46EA) by Juan Villarreal and Emiliano Messiez was released in June 2026. Meredith has taught and performed in more than 40 cities worldwide, including Istanbul, Sydney and Byron Bay (Australia), Gdansk and Brzeg (Poland), Belo Horizonte (Brazil), Beirut (Lebanon), Nicosia (Cyprus), Vancouver and Montreal (Canada), and more than 30 cities in the United States. Meredith has completed a Diploma in Tango at the University of Buenos Aires in Argentina. She has also completed [Dr. Madeleine Hackney](https://med.emory.edu/departments/medicine/profile/?u=MEHACKN)'s Teacher Training in Adapted Tango Classes for Older Individuals and Those with Movement Disorders through [Tango Mercurio](https://www.tangomercurio.org/) and [MDT Education Solutions](https://www.mdteducationsolutions.com/pages/about). Meredith is a member of the Latin Grammy Academy of Recording Artists. In July 2024, Meredith became one of very few foreigners in the world to be named an Honorary Academic of the National Academy of Tango in Buenos Aires, a program of the Argentine Ministry of Culture and Department of Education. She is overwhelmed by this unexpected honor.

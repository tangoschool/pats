Milonga Qilombo: Saturday, January 25

**Saturday, January 25** Bandoneonist Raul Jaurena played with Astor Piazzolla. He has been designated a "Living Treasure" by his native Uruguay. He is also the winner of a Latin Grammy Award. We are beyond thrilled to have him play in our studio for the first time! **8:30-9:30 pm:** All Levels Tango Class - Andres & Meredith **9:30 pm-2 am (or later):** Milonga Qilombo, featuring LIVE MUSIC by Raul Jaurena (bandoneon) and Emiliano Messiez (piano). Guest DJ Maria Aldrete. **Pricing** Pre-registration is now closed, but you can still pay at the door. The cost is $25 and includes the class and milonga. Full-time college and grad students get 20% off. **Location:** Philadelphia Argentine Tango School, 2030 Frankford Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125 *If you are having trouble finding parking within two blocks of the studio, then drive three blocks north to the intersection where York, Trenton and Frankford come together. There is always ample, free parking next to the Hackett School, on both York and Trenton. It is pull-in parking also, so you will not have to parallel park. The three-block walk is well-lit and well-populated. And we will find someone to walk with you to your car when you are ready to leave, if you don't feel comfortable walking by yourself.* If you enjoy this event, consider joining us at [Tango La Nacional](https://www.tangolanacional.com/) in New York City on Thursday, January 30 for a super-fun night. Andres and Meredith will teach and perform. Emiliano and Raul will play live for dancing. And we'll be celebrating Emiliano's birthday!

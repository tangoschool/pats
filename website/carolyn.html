Carolyn Merritt, Yoga Teacher

Carolyn Merritt (200-hour CYT) began practicing yoga in 2002 and she has led classes for people of all ages as well as bilingual (SP/EN) and chair yoga. Carolyn holds a Ph.D. in anthropology, has a background in ballet and modern dance, and is an avid tango practitioner. She is the author of [Tango Nuevo)](https://www.amazon.com/Tango-Nuevo-Carolyn-Merritt/dp/0813042194) (University Press of Florida, 2012), and she writes about dance and performance for [thINKingDANCE](https://thinkingdance.net/). Hatha Flow is a moderately-paced, breath-centered yoga practice suitable for students of all levels. A typical class consists of postures (incorporating moments of stillness and movement), breath work, visualization, and meditation. Students will build strength and flexibility while moving toward a greater sense of balance, calm, and self-awareness. Questions? Contact Carolyn at merritt.carolyn@gmail.com.

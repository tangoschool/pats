How to Dance to Romantica Milonguera

Join [Kristin Balmer](/kristin) and [Elly Fernandez](/elly) for four nights of classes to help you get ready for September 21, when the "World's Best Tango Orchestra" (*Premios Tango 2019*) comes to Philadelphia, [Orquesta Romantica Milonguera](romantica-milonguera-in-philadelphia-september-21). **Monday, August 19** 7-8 pm: Tango Technique: Exploring size, speed, and level in ochos to [Invierno](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2fv4i1fo8g) & [And I Love Her](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G4f8mNro28E) 8-9:30 pm: Intermediate Tango: Integrating the 4 dance elements (size, speed, level, and order) in ocho combinations to evoke specific emotions in [Invierno](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U2fv4i1fo8g) **Wednesday, August 21** 7:30-9 pm: Int/Advanced Tango: Using the 4 elements of dance to improvise with circular movements to [El Reloj](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PPQ-FnAY-ns) **Monday, August 26** 7-8 pm: Tango Technique: Exploring the 4 dance elements in your walk in [Que te importa que te llore](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XiJAi31hHfk) 8-9:30 pm: Intermediate Tango: Integrating the 4 dance elements in your walk to evoke specific emotions in [Remolino](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fqm-QEcwXkU) **Wednesday, August 28** 7:30-9 pm: Creating a super salida by exploring various levels of movement, inspired by [Solamente Ella](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KoHoknZNiaQ)
